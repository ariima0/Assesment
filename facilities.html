Facilities Overview

The College is located in the attractive and pleasantly refurbished building. The building houses computing laboratories, lecture/tutorial rooms, and its own catering facilities and student work areas. There is also a car park at the back of the building.

Our building covers 9500m², houses over 240 staff members, and provides teaching space for more than 1600 students.

Facilities Include:

  • Wi-fi technology
  • Pool teaching rooms, including classrooms for 25-80 students
  • Specialist faculty facilities
  • A double-height lecture theatre at first and second floor level
  • Dramatic three-storey glass open atrium
  • Meeting rooms
  • Office accommodation
  • Specialist IT facilities
  • Reception desk area
  • Catering outlet
  • Parking for disabled badge holders
  • Cycle racks
  • Gallery
